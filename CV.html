Education

    Ph.D. United States History, Corcoran Department of History, University of Virginia, (in progress, expected 2018)

    Dissertation: "Sounding Southern: Music, Militarism, and the Making of the Sunbelt."
    Advisor: Grace Elizabeth Hale

    My project uses music to analyze the cultural impact of the military-industrial complex on racial, political, and regional identities in the U.S. South since the 1950s.
